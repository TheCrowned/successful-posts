In September 2014 I started studying mathematics at the university of Genoa.

In October 2015 I founded <a href="http://www.ilpiacerediscoprire.it/" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Il Piacere di Scoprire</a>, a not-for-profit to help kids discover the world through science experiments in a fun, concrete and intuitive way. We go around doing workshops with kids and also provide online material for teachers and everybody.

In 2016 I started <a href="http://quickmathintuitions.org/" rel="nofollow">Quick Math Intuitions</a>, where I share my personal intuitions of math topics that occurred to me while studying/struggling to understand something.

I graduated in mathematics in September 2017 with a thesis about the Hilbert curve and its application in turning an image into a sequence of sounds (see <a href="https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=3s7h2MHQtxc" rel="nofollow">3blue1brown</a>). I am now enrolled in a MSc in Computer Science.
